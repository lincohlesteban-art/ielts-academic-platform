Cambridge IELTS Tests 1 to 17

Task 1: The chart below shows the proportions of adults in Canada who own one car, two cars, more than two cars, or who do not own a car.

Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features and make comparisons where relevant.

Write at least 150 words.


Task 2: Some people believe school children should be given multiple short vacations, while others believe they should get one long vacation. Give the advantages of both and express your own point of view.

Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience.

Write at least 250 words.
